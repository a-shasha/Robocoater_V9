# Operator Checklist

## Before run

- Confirm the correct workflow: antisolvent or gas quench.
- Confirm the campaign output folder on the lab PC.
- Confirm camera, spectrometer, pump, spin coater, and gas controls are ready.
- Run the available preflight checks.
- Confirm stop conditions are understood before starting.

## During run

- Monitor motion, fluid delivery, camera capture, spectroscopy capture, and HOLMES responses.
- Record unexpected behavior immediately.
- Stop the run if any stop condition is met.

## After run

- Confirm raw campaign files were written.
- Preserve logs and notes needed for review.
- Do not overwrite or hand-edit generated campaign outputs.
