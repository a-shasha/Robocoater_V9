# Stop Conditions

Stop the run immediately if any of the following occur:

- Unexpected hardware motion or failure to move as commanded.
- Solvent leak, spill, blocked line, or unsafe fluid handling condition.
- Pump, spin coater, camera, spectrometer, relay, or gas control failure.
- Missing, corrupt, or clearly invalid data capture during a required measurement step.
- HOLMES endpoint failure that prevents valid campaign progression.
- Operator is uncertain whether the run is safe to continue.

After stopping, preserve the campaign folder, logs, and operator notes for review.
